Are the elderly take away future generation's opportunity?

Owning a property through retirement funds has certain advantages:
• Once it produce rental income, it is taxed at 15%
• If the property is sold after 12 months, 10% capital gain tax is applied
• Sold within 12 months and under transfer balance cap of A$1.9m, no capital gain tax applied
Unlike the US where it is costly to buy real estate through the IRA, owning a property through superannuation only has a effective tax rate of 10%. And it is quite easy to do so that it is often encouraged. Thus, this SMSF scheme is directly beneficial for the elderly whose superannuation funds are stacked up, making the rich richer.
